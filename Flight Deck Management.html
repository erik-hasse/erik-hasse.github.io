II. Preflight Procedures

B. Flight Deck Management

Objective

To determine that the applicant exhibits satisfactory knowledge, risk management, and skills associated with safe flight deck management practices.

Knowledge

  1. Passenger briefing requirements, to include operation and required use of safety restraint systems.
    • *S*eatbelts - required during taxi, takeoff, and landing.
    • *A*ir - environmental controls and air sickness.
    • *F*ire extinguisher - location and use.
    • *E*xit, emergencies, and equipment - Door operation and emergency meeting point.
    • *T*raffic and talking - Monitor for traffic and let me know. No talking during taxi, takeoff, and landing, get quite during radio messages.
    • *Y*our questions
  2. Use of appropriate checklists.
  3. Requirements for current and appropriate navigation data.

Risk Management

  1. Improper use of systems or equipment, to include automation and portable electronic devices.
  2. Flying with unresolved discrepancies.

Skills

  1. Secure all items in the flight deck and cabin.
  2. Conduct an appropriate pre-takeoff briefing, to include identifying the PIC, use of safety belts, shoulder harnesses, doors, sterile flight deck, and emergency procedures.
  3. Program and manage the airplane's automation properly.
