II. Preflight Procedures

C. Engine Starting

Objective

To determine that the applicant exhibits satisfactory knowledge, risk management, and skills associated with recommended engine starting procedures.

Knowledge

  1. Starting under various conditions.
  2. Starting the engine by use of external power.
  3. Engine limitations as they relate to starting.

Risk Management

  1. Propeller safety.

Skills

  1. Position the airplane properly considering structures, other aircraft, wind, and the safety of nearby persons and property.
  2. Complete the appropriate checklist.
