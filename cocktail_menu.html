Cocktails

AUTUMN DAIQUIRI

WARMING AND TROPICAL

Gold rum, lime, pineapple, cinnamon, demerara, bitters

  • 2 oz gold rum
  • ½ oz lime juice
  • ½ oz pineapple juice
  • ¼ oz cinnamon syrup
  • ¼ oz demerara syrup
  • 1 dash Angostura bitters
Shake and strain into a coupe glass.

JET PILOT

INTENSE AND SPICED

Dark & overproof rum, lime, cinnamon, falernum, bitters, absinthe

  • 2½ oz Zombie blend rum
  • ½ oz falernum
  • ½ oz lime juice
  • ½ oz grapefruit juice
  • ½ oz cinnamon syrup
  • 1 dash Angostura bitters
  • 4 drops absinthe
Shake and pour into a double old fashioned glass with the ice

LAST WORD

HERBAL AND ELECTRIC

Gin, green Chartreuse, maraschino, lime

  • ¾ oz gin
  • ¾ oz green Chartreuse
  • ¾ oz maraschino liqueur
  • ¾ oz lime juice
Shake and strain into a coupe glass.

PAPER PLANE

BRIGHT AND BITTERSWEET

Bourbon, Aperol, Amaro Nonino, lemon

  • 1 oz bourbon
  • 1 oz Aperol
  • 1 oz Amaro Nonino
  • 1 oz lemon juice
Shake and strain into a coupe glass.

OAXACA OLD FASHIONED

BOOZY AND SMOKY

Tequila, mezcal, agave, bitters

  • 1½ oz tequila
  • ½ oz mezcal
  • 1 tsp agave syrup
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
Stir with ice and strain into a rocks glass over a large ice cube.

GOLDEN HOUR

NON-ALCOHOLIC · VIBRANT AND SPICED

Pineapple, lime, cinnamon, demerara

  • 2 oz pineapple
  • ¾ oz lime juice
  • ½ oz cinnamon syrup
  • ¼ oz demerara
  • 6 drops tiki bitters (Optional, omit for fully NA)
Shake and pour into a highball glass with the ice
