VI. Navigation

D. Lost Procedures

Objective

To determine that the applicant exhibits satisfactory knowledge, risk management, and skills associated with lost procedures and taking appropriate steps to achieve a satisfactory outcome if lost.

Knowledge

  1. Methods to determine position.
  2. Assistance available if lost (e.g. radar services, communications procedures).

Risk Management

  1. Collision hazards, to include aircraft, terrain, obstacles, and wires.
  2. Distractions, loss of situational awareness, or improper task management.
  3. Failure to record times over waypoints.
  4. Failure to seek assistance or declare an emergency in a deteriorating situation.

Skills

  1. Use an appropriate method to determine position.
  2. Maintain an appropriate heading and climb as necessary.
  3. Identify prominent landmarks.
  4. Use navigation systems/facilities or contact an ATC facility for assistance.
