II. Preflight Procedures

F. Before Takeoff Check

Objective

To determine that the applicant exhibits satisfactory knowledge, risk management, and skills associated with the before takeoff check.

Knowledge

  1. Purpose of pre-takeoff checklist items including:
    1. Reasons for checking each item
    2. Detecting malfunctions
    3. Ensuring the airplane is in safe operating condition as recommended by the manufacturer

Risk Management

  1. Division of attention while conducting pre-flight checks.
  2. Unexpected runway changes by ATC.
  3. Wake turbulence.
  4. A powerplant failure during takeoff or other malfunction considering operational factors such as airplane characteristics, runway/takeoff path length, surface conditions, environmental conditions, or obstructions.

Skills

  1. Review takeoff performance.
  2. Complete the appropriate checklist.
  3. Position the airplane appropriately considering other aircraft, vessels, and wind.
  4. Divide attention inside and outside the flight deck.
  5. Verify that engine parameters and airplane configuration are suitable.
